# Fieldwork Plan: May 20, 2026

## Objective

Gather enough business, finance, operations, and data context to define the MVP for a residential finance automation and dashboard system.

## People To Interview

- Owner
- Property manager or managers
- Finance department
- Operations team
- Any person currently maintaining spreadsheets or reports

## Data To Collect

- Room list
- Occupancy/vacancy tracker
- Income records
- Expense records
- Existing reports
- Dashboard examples if any
- Spreadsheet templates
- Manual reconciliation notes
- Monthly or weekly reporting process

## Questions To Answer By End Of Day

- What are the most painful manual workflows?
- Which reports are required every day, week, and month?
- Which data sources are trusted?
- Which spreadsheet columns are inconsistent or hard to clean?
- Who updates room status?
- Who updates income and expenses?
- Who approves or reviews financial summaries?
- What does the owner need to see first?
- What should the first MVP automate?
