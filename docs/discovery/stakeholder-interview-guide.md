# Stakeholder Interview Guide

## Owner

- What decisions do you want the dashboard to help you make?
- What numbers do you check most often?
- What reports do you currently receive?
- What is hard to trust in the current data?
- What would make this system successful for you?

## Managers

- How do you currently know which rooms are vacant or occupied?
- How often does room status change?
- What manual reports do you prepare?
- What delays or errors happen most often?
- Which operational problems should the system surface?

## Finance Department

- What income categories do you track?
- What expense categories do you track?
- How do you reconcile payments or records?
- What spreadsheets or systems are used today?
- What exports or formats are required?
- What data fields are mandatory?

## Operations Team

- What room updates do you make day to day?
- How do you report maintenance or room readiness?
- What information do you need from finance or managers?
- What repetitive tasks should be automated?

## Closing Questions For Everyone

- What is the most painful part of the current process?
- What errors happen repeatedly?
- What should not change?
- What would you want in the first usable version?
