## 1. Physical Environment & Operations
- **Property Size:** 360 existing rooms; expansion of 120 rooms underway (Total: 480).
- **Room Types:** 
    - **Single:** 4x6m (24 sqm), sleeps 2.
    - **Suite:** Approx. 2x size of Single, includes living area.
- **Client Base:** 60-70% factory contracts (bulk rentals of 20-50 rooms). Individual walk-ins make up the rest.
- **Current Billing:** Using dedicated external software (manual link required).
- **Deposit Policy:** High-value deposit collected upfront (separate from rent). This deposit is used as a "Universal Buffer" to cover:
    - Monthly utilities (Electricity/Water).
    - Cleaning expenses.
    - Maintenance/Damages discovered during or at end of stay.
    - Excess billing: If costs exceed the deposit, tenants are billed for the difference.

## 2. Key Observations (Pain Points)
- **Deposit Reconciliation:** Calculating the final refund is extremely tedious. Khun Aom must manually cross-reference repair logs, utility readings, and cleaning schedules against the initial deposit.
- **Maintenance Tracking:** Current logs are in Excel. Hard to track history per room or identify repeating failures (e.g., specific AC units).
- **Manual Workflows:** Daily ops are paper-based or manual Excel entry. High risk of data silos.
- **Maintenance Culture:** Currently reactive ("Wait until it breaks"). Goal is to move to **Preventive Maintenance** (e.g., scheduled AC cleaning every 6 months).
