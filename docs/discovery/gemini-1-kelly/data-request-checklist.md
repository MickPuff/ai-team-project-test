# Data Request Checklist (Kelly's Part - Gemini 1)

*Don't leave the field visit without these (or photos of them).*

## 📊 Spreadsheets & Templates
- [ ] **Room/Asset Master List:** (Columns: Room #, Type, Standard Rate, Status)
- [ ] **Current Occupancy Tracker:** (How they see who is in which room right now)
- [ ] **Income/Receipt Log:** (Sample of how they record money coming in)
- [ ] **Expense/Voucher Log:** (Sample of how they record money going out)
- [ ] **Bank Reconciliation Template:** (How they check if the cash matches the books)

## 📈 Reports
- [ ] **Sample Monthly Owner Report:** (The "Final" document)
- [ ] **Sample Manager Report:** (Weekly or daily operational updates)
- [ ] **The "Late Payment" List:** (How they track who owes money)

## 📋 Physical Artifacts
- [ ] **Photos of Paper Logs:** (If they use guest books or maintenance clipboards)
- [ ] **Photos of Whiteboards:** (In the office where they track status)

## ⚙️ Technical Context
- [ ] **List of existing software:** (Accounting soft, CRM, or just Microsoft 365)
- [ ] **User count:** (How many people need to login to the new system?)
