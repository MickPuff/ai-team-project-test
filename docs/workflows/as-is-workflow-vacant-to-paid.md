# "As-Is" Workflow: Bo Thong Residence
**Workstream:** WS3 (Business Process)
**Lead:** Kelly (Gemini 1)

## Current Process: Vacant Room -> Paid Occupancy

### 1. Inquiry & Booking
- **Actor:** Factory HR / Individual Tenant.
- **Action:** Contact Management via phone or WeChat.
- **Tool:** Manual check of room availability (Excel or mental map).
- **Pain Point:** No real-time visibility for the whole team.

### 2. Contract & Move-In
- **Actor:** Khun Aom / Admin.
- **Action:** Issue physical contract. Record tenant details in Excel.
- **Tool:** Microsoft Word (Template), Excel.
- **Pain Point:** Contract dates and renewals are tracked manually; easy to miss expiration.

### 3. Maintenance (During Stay)
- **Actor:** Tenant -> Intermediary (China) -> Manager -> Maintenance Staff.
- **Action:** Tenant reports issue via WeChat to middleman. Middleman notifies Manager. Manager tells Maintenance.
- **Tool:** WeChat, Line, Verbal communication.
- **Pain Point:** Severe language barrier. Information loss through middlemen. No history log for repairs.

### 4. Billing & Invoicing
- **Actor:** Finance / Admin.
- **Action:** Enter data into external Invoicing software. Print/Send PDF.
- **Tool:** External Software, Email.
- **Pain Point:** Data is siloed. Doesn't link directly to maintenance costs or occupancy trends.

### 5. Move-Out & Inspection
- **Actor:** Maintenance / Manager.
- **Action:** Inspect room. Note damages. Update Excel to "Available".
- **Tool:** Paper checklist, Excel.
- **Pain Point:** Historical damage records are hard to find during the next inspection.

## Proposed "To-Be" Transformation
- **Centralized Room DB:** Real-time status for all staff.
- **Automated Contracts:** System alerts for upcoming expirations.
- **QR Maintenance Portal:** Direct Chinese-to-Thai translated reports.
- **Unified Reporting:** Linking occupancy, maintenance costs, and revenue.
