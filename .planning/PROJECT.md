# Project: Residential Finance Automation

## Summary

Build a back office automation system for a residential property company. The system should make finance and operations data easier to collect, clean, track, analyze, and summarize.

## Users

- Owner of the residential property
- Managers
- Finance department
- Operations team

## Business Goals

- Reduce manual spreadsheet work
- Improve room vacancy and occupancy visibility
- Track income and expenses more reliably
- Provide useful summaries for business decisions
- Standardize reporting across owner, finance, management, and operations workflows

## Discovery Date

Primary information gathering is planned for May 20, 2026.

## Known Domains

- Residential room/property operations
- Finance tracking
- Spreadsheet/data cleanup
- Reporting
- Business dashboarding
- Back office workflow automation

## Team Roles

- Codex: integration lead and architecture coordinator
- Gemini 1: business process and operations discovery
- Gemini 2: database, spreadsheet, and data cleaning discovery
- Gemini 3: dashboard, reporting, and KPI discovery

## Current Assumptions

- Existing workflows likely rely on spreadsheets or manually updated records.
- There are multiple stakeholder groups with different reporting needs.
- The MVP should focus on one complete operational-finance workflow before broader automation.
- Actual requirements should be finalized after May 20, 2026 discovery.

## Unknowns To Resolve

- Current source data formats
- Data quality problems
- Required dashboards and reports
- User permissions
- Existing tools and software
- Reporting frequency
- Definitions of vacancy, occupancy, income, expense, and profit
- Required historical data migration
