# Project: Residential Finance Automation

## Summary

Build a back office automation system for residential property finance, room occupancy, reporting, and operational summaries. The system should make business data easier to collect, clean, track, analyze, and present to the owner and team.

## Core Problem Statements

### Residential Back Office

Manual management of room occupancy, billing, income, and expenses leads to data silos, delays in reporting, and high operational friction for the owner and finance team.

### Reporting And Data Quality

Current spreadsheet and manual reporting workflows make it hard to trust data, compare performance, and quickly answer basic questions about vacant rooms, income, expenses, and operational issues.

## Business Goals

- **Reduce Manual Entry:** Reduce repeated spreadsheet work and manual consolidation.
- **Improve Visibility:** Give the owner and team clear status for occupancy, finance, and operations.
- **Increase Trust:** Standardize definitions and reduce inconsistent reporting.
- **Standardize Reporting:** Create dashboard and report views for owner, finance, management, and operations.

## 7-Day Deliverables

1. **As-Is Workflow:** Map from check-in or room assignment to monthly reporting.
2. **Data Inventory:** List required tables and spreadsheet sources for rooms, tenants, payments, expenses, and maintenance.
3. **Dashboard Mockup:** Owner's executive view.
4. **Pain Point List:** Document manual tasks for prioritized automation.
5. **MVP Recommendation:** Define the first usable scope for back office automation.
