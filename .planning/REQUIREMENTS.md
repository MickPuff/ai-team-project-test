# Requirements

Status: Draft before discovery.

## Core Capabilities

- Track residential rooms and current occupancy status
- Track vacant and occupied room counts
- Track income by room, period, and category
- Track expenses by category, vendor, date, and property area
- Import or clean spreadsheet data
- Generate owner, manager, finance, and operations summaries
- Provide dashboard KPIs for business monitoring

## User Roles

### Owner

- Needs high-level business summaries
- Needs income, expense, profit, occupancy, and trend visibility
- Needs exceptions and risks surfaced clearly

### Manager

- Needs room and operations status
- Needs visibility into vacancies and operational problems
- Needs regular reports without manual consolidation

### Finance

- Needs reliable income and expense records
- Needs reconciliation support
- Needs exportable summaries

### Operations

- Needs room status, maintenance signals, and daily action lists
- Needs simple update workflows

## Draft MVP Scope

- Room registry
- Occupancy status
- Income/expense records
- Spreadsheet import template
- Summary dashboard
- Basic reports

## Out Of Scope Until Confirmed

- Online payment collection
- Tenant-facing portal
- Lease contract management
- Full accounting system replacement
- Mobile app

## Acceptance Criteria Draft

- Users can see current occupied and vacant room counts
- Users can enter or import income and expenses
- Users can view monthly financial summary
- Users can filter dashboard by month and property area
- Finance can export a report for review
- Data definitions are documented and consistent
